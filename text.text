 Integral Liquid Waterproofing Compound for Concrete & Plaster
 Dr. Fixit Dr.proof LW+ is a specially formulated integral liquid
 waterproofing compound made from high-quality plasticising 
agents, polymers, and active additives. It is used as a waterproof 
additive for cement concrete, mortar, and plaster. The compound
 enhances the cohesiveness of concrete, reduces segregation
 minimizes shrinkage cracks, and provides superior waterproofing
 and durability to structures.
